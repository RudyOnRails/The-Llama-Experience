Llama Riding Tours

A peaceful way to feel the breeze against your face as you and your llama companion travel up the many hills around the farm. Explore the forest as your guide describes the farm and explains its purpose. A guide will be assigned to you and your group and will be available the day you check into the tour. The guide will also review how to mount and ride a llama. We recommend your bring a waterbottle or any other neccessities you may require, for this is a fairly long tour. A waiver is required.
