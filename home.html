Our Passion

We would like to share the beauty of these majestic creatures with everyone. Therefore, you could book one of our llama adventure tours today in the soon to be available contact us tab! Our llama adventure tours are designed specificly to produce the feeling of tranquility and peacefulness whilst traveling with your llama companions. We strive to resurrect the spirit of youth in each of our customers and we strive to continue Bobra Goldsmith's legacy after she passed away on December 7th, 2010. As a result, we have established this site to show our customers the wonders of these creatures and to show them our three splendid and interesting tours.
