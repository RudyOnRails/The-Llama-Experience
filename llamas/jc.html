J.C.

  • Caretaker: Jordan
  • Age: 7 years old

J.C. was one of our donated llamas. He is almost exactly like Gerald and we believe they are the greatest two llama friends in the farm. J.C. exercises caution around people, but isn't all too curious around them. We do get many fantastic remarks of J.C.'s appearance and behavior. Many claim that he is calm and keeps the experience delightfully tranquil. Overall, a customer should appreciate the presence of J.C. on this farm.
