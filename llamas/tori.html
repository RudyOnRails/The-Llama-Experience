Tori

  • Caretaker: Hailey
  • Age: Five years old

Tori is one of our many show llamas here at the farm. She had been magnificently bred to have a coat of fur to that actually looks like she's wearing a coat or some form of clothing! But really it's just her fur. Tori is another one of our curious, lovely llamas. She does enjoy the presence of new people and customers. We don't normally use her in any of our riding tours, but we did make her a primary llama as she's won first play five times in the regional llama show. She truly is a spectacular llama.
