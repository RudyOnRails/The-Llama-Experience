Gerald

  • Caretaker: Hannah
  • Age: Eight years old

Gerald is a very sensitive and gentle llama. He does enjoy the company of new people and customers. His curiosity has no bounds when it comes to the presence of new people, so it is recommended you use some caution around Gerald. Gerald is in no way dangerous except for his colossal size. However, if Gerald feels threatened by any person's presence he will spit. Other than that, Gerald is a very loving llama and is full of compassion and curiosity. We guarantee that every customer will enjoy his humble existence at this farm. He truly is a crucial factor in the development in this program and our tours.
