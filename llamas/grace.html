Grace

  • Caretaker: Megan
  • Age: Nine years old

We named her Grace because she is just the most graceful and peaceful llama any person can ask for. She's gentle when it comes to interacting with other people and she is exceedingly careful when it comes to not trying to hurt anyone. Grace enjoys prancing in the fields even if her long hair interferes or becomes a nuisance, she still continues to prance gracefully in the field. Grace is one of our show llamas meaning that we bred her for show, therefore if you ever gaze upon her glorious, silky white fur through your own eyes you would be absolutely stunned by how well we took care of it. Overall, Grace is definetely one of the most magnificent show llamas you definetely have to see during your visit here.
