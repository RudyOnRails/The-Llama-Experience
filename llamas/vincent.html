Vincent

  • Caretaker: Philip
  • Age: 13 years old

Vincent is one of our most praised riding llamas out here in the farm. Whenever anyone rode him they claimed it was like riding on a cloud. Others also claim that it reminded them of their father giving them a ride on their back when they were children. Vincent is determined to please and will take excellent care of his riders and will exercise caution when traversing rough terrain. Other than being one of the best riding llamas in our farm, Vincent has pristine quality cream fur that is immensely soft and fluffy. We guarantee our customers will love him.
